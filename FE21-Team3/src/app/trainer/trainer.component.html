Theodora Patkos

Theodora Patkos, born and raised in South Africa, is a trainer at Codefactory. In addition to training as a psychotherapist , Theodora has completed the full-stack developer course at Codefactory, expanding her portfolio. She is enthusiastic about the challenge of mastering technical languages , creating websites for different projects and applying her creativity. As a trainer she is happy to contribute with her teaching experiences with her focus on accessibility . Among other things, she develops and trains the courses for deaf developers .
